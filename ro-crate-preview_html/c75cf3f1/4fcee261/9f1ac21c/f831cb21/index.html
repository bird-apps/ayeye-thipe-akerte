Tawny Frogmouth
Podargus strigoides

Thipe uringe. Uringe alengke anthurre iwelhentye akngerre, nhenge tyerrtyele apeke ampele apeke areme-ketye. Arne akertnele em anerle, parrape aretye-akenhe anthurre ilelhemele. nhenge tyerrtye arle apeke ampele arlke areme-ketyenge. Arne antyerrke arteke em anerle-anerle, akngelhetye-akenhe anthurre, nhenge tyerretyele arlke ampele arlke aretyekenhe anetyenhenge. Itne arne kwele aretyenhenge.

The tawny frogmouth is well camouflaged, so people and children can't see it. It sits up in a tree and doesn't show itself and people and children can't notice it. It sits there like a dry stick, not moving, so people and children can't see it. They think it's just a stick.
